24th/July/2020


I am Yujin Jiang, a Taiwanese freelance product designer working in furniture, lighting, and space design. I have expertise in product design, product development, and photography. Alongside my design practice, I have also cultivated knowledge and skills in graphic design, web design, and branding. Before my Master's studies at Konstfack in Stockholm (2012), I worked at the intersection of multiple design disciplines in product design and graphic design in Taiwan and China. Following my Masters at Konstfack, I co-founded in 2015 HAHA Studio — a design studio focusing in the edition of light products. I am available for requests at: info@yujin.design
