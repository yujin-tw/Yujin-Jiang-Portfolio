Rainbow Lamp is a lamp that transcribe the chubby proportions of industrial elbow tubes produced for the boat and marine industry into a refined lighting piece for the home interior. The thickened, childlike shape with its rounded terminations gives the Rainbow Lamp an adorable character that trigger a positive emotional response. It is made to provide the home environment and the workplace with some playful charm.
