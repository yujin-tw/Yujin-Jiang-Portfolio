With the development of the studio’s work and the expansion of its services, HAHA studio was in need of a new visual identity. To reposition the studio and communicate about the work within an identified segment of the design business, I have redesigned HAHA studio identity with the assistance of designer Anna Heck. My contribution included the conception of a new logo, HAHA studio’s visual style guide, HAHA studio’s website as well as art direction for photography and social media contents.
