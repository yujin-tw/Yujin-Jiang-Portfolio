Alia is the Latin word for “Change”. It is a candle holder that does exactly that. It consists of two basic forms (branch and base), that can be built into various compositions. It takes inspiration from the traditional Swedish Jul Ljusstacke – a candle bridge that sits in most of Swede’s windows during the Christmas time. Alia is playful and all about creating free compositions: one branch works alone as a single candle holder, and when multiplied, they can create a much more impressive candle bridge with a modern character.
